# 2022 Chevrolet Corvette Stingray

## Summary
The 2022 Chevrolet Corvette Stingray (C8) marks a historic mid-engine evolution, delivering supercar-level performance at an incredible price point.

## Advantages
- Supercar acceleration and mid-engine cornering precision
- Unbeatable performance-per-dollar ratio

## Disadvantages
- Thick rear pillars cause large blind spots
- Aggressive cockpit divider wall can feel restrictive for passengers
