# 2023 Porsche 911 Carrera

## Summary
The Porsche 911 Carrera is the gold standard for high-performance sports cars, offering classic rear-engine dynamics and daily-drivable luxury.

## Advantages
- Flawless, precise steering and unmatched handling dynamics
- Incredibly versatile as a high-performance track car and daily driver

## Disadvantages
- Extremely high purchase price and expensive optional add-ons
- Virtually unusable rear seats for adult passengers
