# 2024 BMW i4

## Summary
The BMW i4 is an all-electric executive sedan that perfectly blends classic premium driving dynamics with modern electric vehicle refinement.

## Advantages
- Superb, athletic handling that stays true to BMW heritage
- Quiet, high-end luxury cabin with advanced dual-screen infotainment

## Disadvantages
- Rear seat legroom and trunk space are compromised by the gas-car platform share
- Heavier weight impacts tight-corner agility compared to gas 3-Series
