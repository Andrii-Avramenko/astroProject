# 2021 Honda Civic

## Summary
The Honda Civic is globally recognized for its exceptional efficiency, agile handling, and bulletproof reliability.

## Advantages
- Very affordable entry price and excellent resale value
- Highly practical trunk layout and nimble city driving manners

## Disadvantages
- Base models lack premium engine pep
- Cabin experiences elevated road noise at highway speeds
