# 1969 Ford Mustang

## Summary
The 1969 Ford Mustang is an absolute American muscle car icon, famous for its aggressive styling and powerful classic V8 engine options.

## Advantages
- Timeless, aggressive muscle car aesthetics and high collector value
- Raw, nostalgic mechanical driving experience and deep V8 sound

## Disadvantages
- Lacks modern safety features, fuel efficiency, and driver assists
- Requires frequent maintenance and vintage upkeep
