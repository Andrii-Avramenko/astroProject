# 2025 Hyundai Ioniq 5

## Summary
The Hyundai Ioniq 5 is a futuristic electric crossover featuring retro-inspired design cues, ultra-fast 800V charging, and an open layout.

## Advantages
- Blazing fast charging speed (10% to 80% in under 18 minutes on 350kW)
- Unique, eye-catching futuristic exterior and lounge-like interior

## Disadvantages
- Lacks a rear window wiper, impacting visibility in wet or snowy weather
- Real-world highway driving range drops significantly in cold climates
