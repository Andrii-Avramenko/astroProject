# 2022 Toyota Camry

## Summary
The Toyota Camry is a staple of reliability and comfort. The 2022 model continues its legacy as a top-selling midsize sedan, offering balanced driving dynamics and a practical interior.

## Advantages
- Excellent fuel economy and proven long-term reliability
- Comfortable, spacious cabin with intuitive controls

## Disadvantages
- Engine noise can be noticeable under hard acceleration
- Interior design is practical but feels basic compared to premium rivals
